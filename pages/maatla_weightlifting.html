Weight Lifting

Weight lifting is a strength training activity that involves lifting weights to build muscle mass, increase strength, and improve overall fitness. It targets specific muscle groups and allows for progressive overload, leading to enhanced physical performance.

Whether you're a beginner or an experienced lifter, weight lifting provides various exercises and techniques to help you achieve your fitness goals. Explore different lifting programs, learn proper form, and challenge yourself to reach new levels of strength and fitness.
