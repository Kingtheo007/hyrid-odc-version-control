Calisthenics

Calisthenics is a form of exercise that utilizes your body weight for resistance. It involves a variety of movements and exercises aimed at building strength, flexibility, and endurance. The beauty of calisthenics lies in its simplicity and the ability to perform exercises anywhere without the need for specialized equipment.

Whether you're a beginner or an advanced fitness enthusiast, calisthenics offers a scalable and effective way to improve your overall fitness level. Explore different bodyweight exercises, progressions, and routines to challenge yourself and achieve your fitness goals.
